What happens when you flag?

  • A ##TEST_ACCOUNT## marker is added to your Circles profile metadata
  • Analytics dashboards filter out flagged accounts from growth metrics
  • Trust Membership Services (TMSs) skip flagged accounts when adding to groups
  • The flag is stored on-chain via IPFS — you sign a transaction to prove ownership
  • You can remove the flag at any time by reconnecting and clicking "Remove"
